> **Owner:** chro + cfo + clo
> **Approver:** board
> **Chain role:** Grants key C-levels and senior ICs the right to buy company shares in the future based on milestones and tenure (vesting), aligning employee and shareholder interests.
> **Status:** DRAFT
> **Gate:** G-equity (requires board resolution before any options are granted)
> **Consistency:** must not contradict CHAIRMAN_LETTER.md or VISION.md.

---

## Purpose

The Stock Option Plan ("Plan") authorizes the company to grant eligible employees and contractors the right to acquire shares in the future at a pre-set exercise price, subject to vesting conditions. The Plan aligns the long-term financial interests of key contributors with those of shareholders, retains talent without excessive immediate cash outlay, and creates a transparent, auditable equity incentive structure.
