> **Owner:** clo + chairman + ceo
> **Approver:** board
> **Chain role:** Regulates the relationship among owners and the Chairman — board voting rules, share pre-emption rights, and the quorums required for critical decisions (dismissing a C-level, selling the company). Prevents ego conflicts between leadership and the board.
> **Status:** DRAFT
> **Gate:** G-legal (must be executed before any equity is transferred or new investor admitted)
> **Consistency:** must not contradict CHAIRMAN_LETTER.md or VISION.md.

---

## Purpose

The Shareholders Agreement governs the rights and obligations of the company's shareholders in relation to one another and to the Board. It supplements the Articles of Incorporation by setting out voting rules, share transfer restrictions, pre-emption rights, and the specific quorums required for decisions that affect the company's ownership structure, leadership, or fundamental direction. It is the primary instrument for preventing governance disputes.
