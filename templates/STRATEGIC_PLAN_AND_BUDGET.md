# STRATEGIC PLAN AND BUDGET

> **Owner:** ceo + cfo
> **Approver:** board
> **Chain role:** Translates the CEO's vision into revenue targets and spend limits for the year; each C-level receives a documented slice of the budget and the delivery target to demand from its ICs.
> **Status:** DRAFT
> **Gate:** G-finance (must be approved before the fiscal year begins or, for the first cycle, before operational spending exceeds 30 days)
> **Consistency:** must not contradict CHAIRMAN_LETTER.md or VISION.md.

---

## Purpose

The Strategic Plan and Budget is the single binding document that connects the company's long-term vision to this fiscal year's resource allocation and delivery commitments. It cascades the board-approved revenue target down to each C-level as a departmental budget and delivery mandate. No department may spend beyond its approved budget line without the procedure defined in AUTHORITY_MATRIX.md.
